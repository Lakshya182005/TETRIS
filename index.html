TETRIS INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to Tetris!

Objective: Arrange falling blocks (Tetrominoes) to form complete lines. When a line is complete, it disappears and you earn points. The game ends when the blocks reach the top.

Controls:

  • Arrow Down: Move piece down faster
  • Arrow Left: Move piece left
  • Arrow Right: Move piece right
  • Arrow Up: Rotate piece

Scoring:

  • Clearing 1 line = +10 points
  • Clearing 2 lines = +30 points
  • Clearing 3 lines = +50 points
  • Clearing 4 or more lines = +100 points
